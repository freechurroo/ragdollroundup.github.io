Site rules

1. This site is only for people 16 or older.

2. We do not tolerate political talk or themes on any part of the website.

3. Advertising any breeding business without proper authentication approved by staff will result in an IP ban. See our breeding advertisement permission rules and form further down the page.

4. You must be 18 or older to participate in any meet ups we will host.

5. Do not post for health advice, we recommend you talk to your vetrinarian for proper care.

6. Artists posting commission ads must link other social media profiles along with the advertisements.

7. Harassment, hate speech, sexual content, or other inappropriate content will result in two temporary bans before a permanent IP ban.

Site history

Ragdoll roundup was created in 2023 for a college students project, but the site struck a cord with ragdoll lovers.

Ragdoll roundup has been a website for a short period now, gathering all ragdoll lovers and owners to share the cutest pictures of their babies. We encourage a loving and safe platform to find other enthusiasts and friends. With all the cuddly love you can find, Ragdoll Roundup welcomes anyone onto our cute little site!

On this site, you can share pictures of your babies and look at all the pictures of other fluffy cats that you want! We even partner with breeders so others seeking their first ragdoll or another addition to their clouds can easily find great places to get kittens. Our breeders are required to be TICA certified and need to have at least a 4 star average review.

We also do meet ups for site members 18 and older. Currently, we only do these meetups in the United States. Giveaways and games are usually hosted at these meetups.

More information on meetups here.

Breeding ads approval

All breeders that we allow to advertise on our site must present their TICA certification to us. We also require:

- At least a 4 star google review average

- At least 20 proved adoptions from your business (using presentable paperwork)

- You have your own website
